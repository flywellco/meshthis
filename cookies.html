Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Compliant with EU eCookie Directive and GDPR

✅
We use zero tracking cookies

MeshThis does not use cookies of any kind. No analytics cookies, no advertising cookies, no session cookies. The only browser storage we use is localStorage — which stays on your device and never leaves it.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device. They are used for everything from keeping you logged in to tracking you across websites for advertising. MeshThis uses none of these.

What We Use Instead: localStorage

The only browser storage MeshThis uses is the browser's built-in localStorage API. Unlike cookies, localStorage data never leaves your device, is never transmitted to our servers, and is not used for tracking or profiling.

We use Plausible Analytics — a privacy-first tool that uses no cookies, does not track individuals, anonymises IP addresses, and is GDPR compliant without requiring a consent banner.

EU eCookie Directive Compliance

Because MeshThis uses no cookies at all — and Plausible Analytics uses no cookies — we are fully compliant with the EU eCookie Directive without requiring a cookie consent popup. The storage notice banner is purely informational.

How to Clear Your Data

Your localStorage data can be cleared at any time. Clearing it will reset your daily conversion counter and sign you out of any active session.

✓ All MeshThis localStorage data cleared.

  • Chrome / Edge: DevTools (F12) → Application → Local Storage → meshthis.com
  • Firefox: DevTools (F12) → Storage → Local Storage → meshthis.com
  • Safari: Develop → Web Inspector → Storage → Local Storage
